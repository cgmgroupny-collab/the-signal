Editorial Policy & Affiliate Disclosure

How we review products, handle affiliate relationships, and maintain independence.

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Affiliate Disclosure

The Signal contains affiliate links. When you click on a link and sign up for a product or service, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we fund our operations and keep our content free.

Common affiliate relationships include:

  • AI tool subscriptions (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
  • Cryptocurrency exchanges (Coinbase, Kraken, Gemini, etc.)
  • Sports betting platforms (Bovada, BetOnline, etc.)
  • Software and SaaS tools

All affiliate links use the rel="nofollow noopener sponsored" attribute as required by search engine guidelines.

Editorial Independence

Affiliate commissions never influence our rankings, ratings, or recommendations. Our process:

  1. We choose what to review. Companies do not pay for reviews or request coverage. We select products based on reader interest and search demand.
  2. We test everything ourselves. Every product is tested by our editorial team with real accounts, real money, and real usage. We do not rely solely on press releases or marketing materials.
  3. Rankings reflect our testing. Products are ranked based on our evaluation criteria (documented in each article's methodology section). We have ranked products lower despite higher affiliate commissions.
  4. No pre-publication review. Companies do not see our articles before publication. We may contact companies for factual verification (pricing, features) but never for editorial approval.

Our Review Process

Every review follows a structured methodology:

  • We create real accounts and use the product for a meaningful period (typically 2-4 weeks minimum).
  • We document specific criteria before testing begins to avoid bias.
  • We include both pros and cons for every product â€” no product is perfect.
  • We verify pricing, features, and claims against official sources.
  • We disclose the date of testing and commit to updating articles when products change.

Content Updates

Products change. Prices increase. Features get added or removed. We commit to:

  • Displaying a "Last Updated" date on every review article.
  • Reviewing and updating articles quarterly at minimum.
  • Marking outdated information clearly when discovered.
  • Publishing correction notices for factual errors.

Risk Disclaimers

For content in regulated industries, we include appropriate disclaimers:

  • Cryptocurrency: Articles include risk warnings about volatility, regulatory uncertainty, and the speculative nature of crypto investments. We are not licensed financial advisors.
  • Sports betting: Articles include age restrictions, responsible gambling resources (NCPG, Gamblers Anonymous), and jurisdictional legality notes. We encourage readers to only bet what they can afford to lose.
  • Financial products: Articles state that content is informational and does not constitute financial advice.

Corrections Policy

If we publish inaccurate information, we will:

  1. Correct the error as quickly as possible.
  2. Add a visible correction notice at the top of the article explaining what was changed and when.
  3. Never silently edit published claims without disclosure.
